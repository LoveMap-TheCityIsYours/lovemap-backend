Privacy Policy

Email Addresses

LoveMap registration requires your email address.

The email address is not used for marketing purposes.

Your email address is not shared with third parties and is stored securely.

Just contact us, and we will delete your LoveMap profile and email address.

Usernames

Username, which is provided by the user during registration is visible to other users of LoveMap.

Please make sure you set your username accordingly, considering your own privacy needs.

Username / Display Name can be changed any time on the Profile Page of LoveMap.

Location

If you enable location access for LoveMap, we will store your last known location anonymously and securely.

We are using this data to provide value-added services for you.
